About Me

Hello! My name is Ryan Kang and I'm currently a senior (Class of 2019) at the University of California San Diego. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics and Computer Science.

With a growing passion in problem solving and programming, I wanted to take on a more hands-on experience by attending LearningFuze, an accelerated web development program that exposed me to modern web development practices. At LearningFuze, I had the opportunity to challenge myself in learning frontend and backend technologies. I loved every single minute of my experience which consisted of daily lectures, hackathons, web-application projects, stand-ups and interactions with other programmers.

In my free time I love cooking chicken alfredo and reading Medium articles on mental health, productivity, and technology.

I am looking for new opportunities that will challenge me and expose me to new areas of software development.
